# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Engineers

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality through testing, validation, and quality assurance processes. They collaborate with developers and product teams to define and verify acceptance criteria.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing
- Report and track defects, verify fixes
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Support release readiness and regression testing

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Provide clear, actionable feedback to developers

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Bug reports and test result summaries
- Collaboration with developers on test coverage

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on user-centered design, ensuring that product interactions are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs. They lead the creation of user experiences that drive customer satisfaction.

### Responsibilities
- Lead wireframing and prototyping sessions
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Provide feedback on feature design and user flows
- Create design systems and style guides
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design practices

### Goals
- Create intuitive and delightful user experiences
- Validate designs through user research and testing
- Maintain design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and insights
- Collaboration with Product Managers on user requirements
- Design handoffs to developers with specifications

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Product Managers to align on user requirements and define features
- Works closely with Developers to implement designs and ensure technical feasibility
- Partners with QA to validate user experience during testing

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers document processes, product features, API references, and user instructions to ensure clarity and consistency for end users and the team. They make complex technical information accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain internal and public-facing documentation
- Help standardize language and structure in project artifacts
- Support onboarding and training efforts
- Document API references, user guides, and process documentation
- Review technical accuracy of documentation

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate, and accessible documentation
- Support user adoption and team productivity
- Maintain documentation quality and consistency

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with subject matter experts
- Collaboration sessions with developers and product teams
- Content planning meetings

### Interactions
- Works with Developers and Project Managers to capture accurate technical content
- Reviews with QA to align on acceptance criteria language and test documentation
- Collaborates with Product Managers on user-facing content

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers ensure reliability, scalability, and security of automation pipelines and deployment infrastructure. They bridge development and operations to enable continuous delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain CI/CD pipelines and cloud infrastructure
- Monitor releases and coordinate incident response
- Implement infrastructure-as-code and observability tooling
- Optimize deployment processes and automation
- Ensure system reliability and performance

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and safe deployments
- Maintain high system uptime and performance
- Automate operational tasks and reduce manual effort

### Typical Communication
- Incident response coordination and postmortems
- Infrastructure planning sessions
- Deployment status and metrics reporting

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Developers on deployment needs and pipeline improvements
- Partners with Project Managers for release planning and incident reporting
- Works with Security Lead on infrastructure security and compliance

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business requirements with technical implementation, supporting the decomposition of high-level goals into actionable specifications. They ensure alignment between business needs and technical solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Document detailed requirements and business logic
- Facilitate stakeholder interviews and requirement gathering
- Define acceptance criteria and create workflow diagrams
- Analyze business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Support change management and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Ensure clear, complete, and actionable requirements
- Bridge communication between business and technical teams
- Support successful project delivery through thorough analysis

### Typical Communication
- Requirement specification documents
- Stakeholder workshops and interviews
- Process flow diagrams and user stories

### Interactions
- Works closely with Product Managers and Project Managers to translate business needs
- Validates development output against requirements with the development team
- Collaborates with UX Designers on user workflows and processes

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach the team in Agile best practices. They serve the team by enabling effective collaboration and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Lead standups, retrospectives, sprint planning, and reviews
- Shield team from scope creep and external disruptions
- Track impediments and facilitate their resolution
- Coach team members on Agile principles and practices
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and effectiveness
- Remove blockers and enable smooth workflow
- Build a high-performing, self-organizing team

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of Agile ceremonies
- Impediment tracking and escalation
- Team coaching and mentoring

### Interactions
- Supports Developers and Project Managers in delivery execution
- Coordinates with Product Managers on backlog prioritization and refinement
- Partners with stakeholders to manage expectations and protect team capacity

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads focus on product and infrastructure security throughout the development lifecycle. They ensure that security is integrated into all phases of project delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Perform threat modeling and security reviews
- Support secure coding practices and compliance requirements
- Conduct security assessments and penetration testing
- Coordinate incident response in collaboration with DevOps and PM
- Provide security training and guidance to the team

### Goals
- Ensure security is built into products from the start
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and risks
- Maintain compliance with security standards

### Typical Communication
- Security review findings and recommendations
- Threat modeling sessions
- Incident response coordination

### Interactions
- Advises Developers and QA on security requirements and testing
- Partners with Project Managers for risk management and security escalation
- Collaborates with DevOps Engineers on infrastructure security and compliance
